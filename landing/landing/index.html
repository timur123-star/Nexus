Why NexusShield

Reliability first. Then everything else.

Most clients look fine until the tunnel hiccups. NexusShield is engineered around the moment things go wrong.

🛡️

OS-level kill-switch

Real firewall enforcement on Windows, macOS and Linux. If the tunnel drops, your true IP never leaks — traffic is blocked, not "best-effort".

🔁

Self-healing watchdog

A native supervisor restarts a crashed or hung core automatically and fails over to your fastest reachable server — no flicker, no manual reconnect.

⚡

Real speed tests

Measure actual download, upload, latency and jitter through the live tunnel — not just a ping. Pick the server that's genuinely fastest.

🧭

Smart routing

Per-domain, per-IP and per-app rules with one-tap scenario profiles (Home · Work · Streaming). Bypass or block exactly what you want.

🔌

Dual-core engine

Sing-box and Xray under one roof, with automatic core selection per protocol. Mux, TLS fragmentation and Reality all supported.

⌘

Built for speed of use

Command palette (⌘K), global hotkeys, mini-mode widget, QR & clipboard import, scheduled subscription updates and a live config editor.
